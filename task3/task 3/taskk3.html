Web Developer Internship Opportunities at Cognifyz Technologies

About the Internship

Our web developer internship program at Cognifyz Technologies offers an immersive experience designed to equip you with practical skills and industry insights. This program spans over 3 months and provides hands-on experience in real-world projects.

Why Join Us?

  • Real-World Experience: Work on actual projects and make tangible contributions.
  • Mentorship: Gain valuable insights and guidance from experienced professionals.
  • Skill Development: Enhance your skills in web development and learn best practices.
  • Networking: Connect with industry experts and build your professional network.

Qualifications

We're looking for candidates who have:

  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Experience with front-end frameworks like React or Vue.js.
  • Basic knowledge of backend technologies such as Node.js or Python is a plus.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and a passion for learning.
  • Good communication skills and the ability to work in a team environment.
